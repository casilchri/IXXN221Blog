My Blog

In this blog I have my work throughout the trimester in this blog along with my group projects work and development.

I coded a simple three paged website with a home page, tutorials page, group work page. The layout of my blog is simply the title, date and reflection accompanied by images placed in a column and grid like structure. When you hover over the images on my blog it glows around the boarder.

My website is responsive and the content stacks on top of each other when the screen size gets smaller. I wanted to use minimal colour on my website so that viewers could focus more on the content and images.
